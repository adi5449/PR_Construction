Our Journey
1996

Founded in Pune

Rohit Goud establishes PR Constructions in Pune, Maharashtra, completing the first landmark commercial project in the region.

2005

Regional Expansion

Growing across Maharashtra and beyond. First major public infrastructure commission wins the State Architecture Excellence Award.

2014

Sustainability Charter

PR Constructions adopts its Regenerative Design Charter, pledging net-positive environmental impact on all new projects.

2024

38+ Landmarks Worldwide

Our portfolio spans 18 countries across 4 continents, with 14 international design awards and counting.
